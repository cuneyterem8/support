Before contacting support

Please include your device model, iOS version, and issue description. Explain what you expected, what happened, and whether restarting the app changed the result.

Local progress

Snake Jack stores gameplay progress and settings locally on your device. The game has no account or cloud synchronization, so deleting the app may remove that local progress.

Optional credits

snake_product1 is an optional Apple consumable that adds 10,000 credits for cosmetic skins. If a completed purchase is not reflected, reopen the app so unfinished delivery can retry, then contact support if needed.
