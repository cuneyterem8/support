Privacy policy

Pulsar Jack Privacy

Pulsar Jack does not collect personal data. The game has no user accounts, no advertising trackers, and no remote analytics.

On-device data

Gameplay progress, settings, and transaction identifiers used to prevent duplicate credit delivery remain on the device. SessionAnalytics is device-only gameplay history and is not remote analytics.

Purchases

Apple processes App Store purchases under Apple’s terms. The app keeps only the on-device transaction state needed to grant credits once and recover unfinished delivery.

Support messages

Support email is used only to respond to messages users choose to send. Contact cuneyterem8@gmail.com if you have a privacy question.
