Before contacting support

Please include your device model, iOS version, and issue description. Explain what you expected, what happened, and whether restarting the app changed the result.

Device-local history

SessionAnalytics is device-only gameplay history. It helps present your activity inside the app and is not sent to a remote analytics service.

Optional credits

Pulsar Jack offers an optional Apple consumable purchase of 20,000 credits. If a completed purchase is not reflected in your balance, restart the app and contact support if the issue continues.
